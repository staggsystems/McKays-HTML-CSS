Hole 6

Men

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 470 yds
  • Handicap: 17

Women

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 400 yds
  • Handicap: 17

This slightly uphill par 5 requires accuracy throughout the hole. Avoid the water hazards and tall tree on the right. The only safe location is on the fairway. Two good shots leave you with a short iron approach to a green sloping from the back to the front.
