Hole 10

Men

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 492 yds
  • Handicap: 6

Women

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 431 yds
  • Handicap: 6

The 10th is a long par 5, initially sloping downhill and then gradually sloping uphill to the green. The green is large, but your approach must be on target or you might find one of the five guardian bunkers.
