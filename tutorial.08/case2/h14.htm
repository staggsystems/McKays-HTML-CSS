Hole 14

Men

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 315 yds
  • Handicap: 13

Women

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 275 yds
  • Handicap: 13

This medium length par 4 plays longer with the wind is blowing against you. If your tee shot finds the fairway, birdies are definite possibility – but remember to add one club length to your approach shot to compensate for the wind.
