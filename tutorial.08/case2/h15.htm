Hole 15

Men

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 520 yds
  • Handicap: 12

Women

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 410 yds
  • Handicap: 12

The 15th requires the golfer to have both length and accuracy off the tee. Watch out for the fairway bunker on your tee shot. Your second shot plays shorter than it appears, due to a favorable downhill slope.
