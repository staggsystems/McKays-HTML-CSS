Hole 2

Men

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 340 yds
  • Handicap: 7

Women

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 280 yds
  • Handicap: 10

A relatively short but deceptively difficult par 4. Your first shot should favor the right side of the fairway. Your second shot is one of the most demanding shots at Willet Creek, hitting into a very shallow green.
