Hole 18

Men

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 530 yds
  • Handicap: 3

Women

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 411 yds
  • Handicap: 3

The course ends with this challenging par 5. A powerful drive will give you a good chance to reach the green in two. Shorter hitters can use the slope of the fairway to position their second shots for a short iron approach to the green. Six bunkers guard this green, making your approach that much tougher.
