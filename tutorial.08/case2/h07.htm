Hole 7

Men

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 330 yds
  • Handicap: 11

Women

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 301 yds
  • Handicap: 11

Your tee shot must avoid the fairway bunker on the right and the cluster of fir trees on the left. The 7th hole is of medium length, but it plays longer, since the last 150 yards are all uphill. The back left pin placements on this green are the most difficult.
