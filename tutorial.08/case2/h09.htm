Hole 9

Men

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 413 yds
  • Handicap: 8

Women

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 368 yds
  • Handicap: 8

This long 4 plays shorter than listed due to a downhill slope. Favor the right side of the fairway for a simple approach shot. When the pin is up front, birdies are plenty. When the pin is in back, settle for par.
