Hole 12

Men

  • Par: 3
  • Distance: 170 yds
  • Handicap: 18

Women

  • Par: 3
  • Distance: 84 yds
  • Handicap: 18

The 12th is a scenic par 3 that forces the golfer to hit across a ravine. Don't be initimated by the hazard, but concentrate on hitting the center of the green. If you can avoid the two front bunkers, you will be a stroke away from birdie.
