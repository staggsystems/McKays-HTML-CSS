Hole 16

Men

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 345 yds
  • Handicap: 2

Women

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 280 yds
  • Handicap: 2

Your tee shot must carry a water hazard and find its way to the fairway that is divided by Willet Creek. After negotiating this hazard, your approach shot flies to an elevated three-tiered green. Sounds tough? It is.
