Hole 11

Men

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 366 yds
  • Handicap: 15

Women

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 341 yds
  • Handicap: 15

The 11th plays slighty downhill, with the wind at your back. Long hitters must avoid the creek that runs 90 yards short of the green. The green runs from left to right. The easiest pin placements are on the right side.
