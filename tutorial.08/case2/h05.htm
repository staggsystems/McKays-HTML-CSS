Hole 5

Men

  • Par: 3
  • Distance: 154 yds
  • Handicap: 16

Women

  • Par: 3
  • Distance: 135 yds
  • Handicap: 16

This par 3 provides one of the most challenging shots at Willet Creek. Your tee shot must carry at least 100 yards to clear a small pond. Avoid the bunkers that surround the back of the green.
